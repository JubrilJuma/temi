Experience Morocco is a leading provider of curated travels and events across Morocco. We offer the very best of Moroccan hospitality with bespoke experiences hosted from the foothills of the Atlas to the soaring Saharan dunes! 

 

Co-founded by two Moroccans who studied and worked in the US, Experience Morocco is steeped in a strong culture of customer service, and we always seek to combine our country’s exotic appeal with world-class care to keep our guests safe and exceed their expectations throughout their stay. Whether you're organizing a small trip with friends or a large corporate retreat, we can tailor-make unique journeys based on your particular needs.

Your Resource for LGBTQ Travel

The International Gay & Lesbian Travel Association was founded in 1983 and is the world's leading network of LGBTQ-welcoming tourism businesses. We provide free travel resources and information while continuously working to promote equality and safety within LGBTQ tourism worldwide. IGLTA’s members include LGBTQ-friendly accommodations, transport, destinations, service providers, travel agents, tour operators, events and travel media located in over 75 countries.

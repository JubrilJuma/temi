Moroccan Golf Getaway

From tee off above the Strait of Gibraltar to playing a round in the palm groves of Marrakech, we invite you to join a once-in-a-lifetime discovery of Morocco through golf. Whether you’re visiting as a couple or traveling with your game partners, you’ll enjoy a selection from 30 courses and resorts located throughout Morocco; places where time slows down and happy moments with friends and family seem to linger longer.


With exquisite designs reflecting the rich architectural history of Morocco, your passion for golf can take you to Casablanca, Marrakech, Essaouira, Agadir, and Tangier where world-class courses await alongside centuries of history and culture.


When you’re not on the course, you can lounge by the pool, enjoy premium Moroccan Hammam and SPA treatments, or take the time to immerse yourself into the local sights, flavours and sounds of Morocco. From the pristine greens to the wide ocean vistas and the winding alleyways of the old medinas, join us for a golf getaway you won’t soon forget.


Golf Destinations

Trip Overview

Morocco is host to a number of incredible golf properties and we look forward to hosting you as you explore the greens across the country. Whether it's a private invitation to the Tikida Golf Palace or arranging a round of golf by night in the palm groves of Marrakech, we can compose a luxury golf holiday and tour personally tailored to your practice. To get started, contact us or scroll through a selection of the destinations we offer.

Essaouira

A small fishing village built by the Portuguese in the 16th century, Essaouira was known as a calm haven amidst the turbulent seas of the Atlantic. You’ll enjoy a stay at the leading five stars Sofitel Mogador Golf and Spa with its exceptional 18-hole course overlooking the ocean.The Mogador course is set to suit all golfing requirements - challenging a golfer's skills even improving the odd handicap. Off the course, you’ll find world-class Spa facilities and tranquil shopping the in heart of the old city just a few minutes away.

Marrakech

Known as the Ochre City, Marrakech will be a sure highlight of your Moroccan adventure. You’ll feel the unique energy of enchanting scents, flavors and colors. Designed by none other than Jack Nicklaus, the Samanah golf course sits at the base of the Atlas Mountains. Take in the orange blossom and eucalyptus scents as you set out for an exceptional round of golf. In the afternoon, explore the labyrinthine network of alleyways in the souk, with thousands of stalls hawking spices, leather, lanterns, and carpets in a dazzling range of designs and colors.

Agadir

Elegant, contemporary and luxurious, the Tikida Golf Palace Hotel features a modern 36-hole golf course built in a stunning location bordered by a forest. The course is lined with bougainvilleas and swaying palm trees, while the sandy beach creates a calming atmosphere. Water Sports can also be arranged from the resort’s private beach.

Tangier

Standing on Africa’s precipice at the crossroads of the Atlantic and Mediterranean, Tangier’s sumptuous views and laidback atmosphere have attracted artists from Matisse to the Rolling Stones. Founded in 1945, Royal Country Club of Tangier is the first golf club in the Mediterranean basin and one of the oldest golf courses in Africa. Renovated in 2001 by Peter Harradine, Royal Country Club of Tangier offers a quality game in a natural and authentic environment located only 3 km away from the city center.

Fes

A World Heritage Site, Fes offers a unique sensory experience with some 10,000 narrow cobblestone streets teeming with ancient mosques, towering minarets, historic palaces, and traditional tanneries. Set just a few minutes away from the medina, Oued Fez offers an 18-hole championship-style golf course that was designed by Jonathan Gaunt. The grassland areas comprise bold, flowing and undulating features with water channels that flood when levels rise in winter and become wetland/marshland during the drier seasons.

Golfer Highlights

Whether you choose to bask in the sun at the foot of the Atlas Mountains, tee off on the coast of the Mediterranean, or play in the heart of imperial cities, you’ll enjoy unmatchable views of the Morocco’s rich landscapes as you set off for each round.

You’ll be playing some of the same courses as the world’s best golfers who visit Morocco each year for leading golf competitions such as the Lalla Aisha Ladies European Tour , the Morocco Matchplay or the Hassan II Golf Trophy.

From the views of the High Atlas to the bay of Tangier, it’s easy to get lost in the surroundings but your Getaway is also about improving your swing! You’ll work with a local pro for individualized attention to improve your game and turn in some of the best scorecards ever.

Connect with Nature

You’ll enjoy golf courses set amongst some of Morocco’s lushest gardens. You’ll enjoy ample time to connect with nature on and off the course with palm trees, cactuses, olive and pine trees perfuming the air with their fragrances from Tangier to Marrakech.

Moroccan Wellness

You will enjoy a luxury package consisting of a Moroccan Hammam and spa treatment including a full body massage of choice, a foot massage, a rebalancing facial and a manicure/pedicure. Professional therapists will be taking the greatest care of you. In between your treatments, iced mint water, mint tea , fresh dried fruits, and little biscuits will be served for a complete sensory celebration

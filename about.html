WHAT WE DO

We create a unique context for connection by building a community of forward-thinking travel professionals.

We are dedicated to uncovering the most rebellious design and lifestyle-led hotel and travel brands and connecting them with leisure, corporate and entertainment buyers whose sole focus is the next generation of creative class traveller.

PROGRAMME

We optimise every opportunity for making, strengthening and reviving industry relationships.

Over four days, our community is brought together through up to 45 one-on-one prescheduled meetings with likeminded brands; thought leadership at Ministry of Ideas; plenty of networking time to catch up with industry friends; and evening events to celebrate our collective spirit.

LOCATION

LE Miami takes place in the Miami Beach Convention Centre.

Not being your standard trade show, we’ve conceived an environment that reflects the rebellious, inventive spirit of the creative class. Taking our cues from the world of art, fashion, music and design, LE Miami fuses exhibitor stands with spaces to network and be inspired, including a DJ booth, bar, art installations and big screens.

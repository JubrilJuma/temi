Destinations

With international airports in all of its main cities, Morocco is easily accessible from the US as well as Europe. The country also offers a choice of several modern convention centres with state-of-the-art facilities for everything from golf roundtables to international conferences. The following slides present a few of cities where we can host your next event.

Casablanca

As the country’s leading economic capital, Casablanca embodies modern Morocco and foreshadows its economic growth. It’s impossible to feel indifferent to this vibrant city. From the Twin Center’s skyscrapers to the imposing Hassan II mosque, Casablanca’s rich architectural heritage can make for a stunning urban backdrop to your event. Whether you want to explore contemporary Moroccan street art or meet with Morocco’s largest banks and industrial businesses, Casablanca is the place to be.

Marrakech

Marrakech is two pulsating cities in one, where modern culture sits just on the other side of 11th century walls. And all this endearing commotion is surrounded by quiet Berber villages in the Atlas mountains just a stone’s throw away. Nicknamed the Ochre City, Marrakech is an established destination for international conferences with several high-profile events hosted there in the last year including the FIFA Club World Cup and the Global Entrepreneurship Summit (headlined by VP Joe Biden).

Tangier

Tangier’s charms have inspired artists, writers and travelers from Henri Matisse to Tennessee Williams. With a beautiful bay overlooking the Strait of Gibraltar, Tangier is just a 2-hour flight from most European capitals and features the largest port in the Mediterranean as well as several world-class resorts to host events and conferences.

Essaouira

The ideal location for a team building offsite, Essaouira’s brilliant blue and white architecture, windswept beaches and easy-going charm provide the perfect backdrop for a memorable trip with colleagues. Behind the 18th century walls are artisan workshops, souks, and winding alleyways that can play host to a life-size game of Treasure Hunt (a popular activity for team-building)

Sample Activities

Tagine Cooking Workshop

From picking your ingredients to shopping for spices, prepare to become a bone fide Moroccan chef with our Tagine cooking workshops! You’ll learn to make your very own Moroccan buffet from salads to tagine and even some local pastries to go with sweet mint tea at the end of your meal!

Jam with a Tribal Band

Gnawa music is a rich repertoire of African mystic songs and rhythms. Its well preserved heritage combines ritual poetry with traditional music and dancing. Jam with a local tribal band under the starry sky of the Sahara Desert or at Jemaa El Fna, Marrakech's Central square for a vibrant team experience!

Camel Ride

Enjoy the quintessential Moroccan team building experience with a group camel ride in the rugged landscapes around Marrakech or the Saharan dunes. For those who haven’t ridden a camel before, the experience of getting on and off the camel is a sensation unlike any other!

Sahara Adventures

Embark on a Saharan adventure to sandboard down the dunes, watch the sunset on camelback, and enjoy a magical night under the stars from your private bedouin camp

Yoga

Whether you’re new to yoga or way up on your handstand, we can organize a group yoga class in the intimacy of your riad for a relaxing break after your working sessions.

Nightlife

With a wide array of international restaurants, clubs, and cabarets featuring world-class entertainment, Morocco’s nightlife offers something for everyone and we can make sure that after working hard by day, your group can enjoy some downtime by night!

Hike in the Atlas Mountains

Morocco’s wealth of landscapes provides the perfect backdrop for a memorable hike through the Atlas mountains, stopping at local Berber villages for a taste of a lifestyle unchanged for centuries.

Destinations

Atlas Mountains

A perennial draw for anyone in search of authentic Berber culture, stunning vistas, and crisp mountain air.

Casablanca

Casablanca, or Casa as it’s know to locals, is Morocco’s cosmopolitan economic hub where traditional Moroccan values meet 21st century energy.

Chefchaouen

Renown for its powder-blue walls, Chefchaouen is a picturesque and highly atmospheric town lodged in the Rif mountains.

Essaouira

With its brilliant Moorish architecture, windswept beaches, and laid back charms, Essaouira is a fantastic escape on Morocco’s Atlantic coast.

Fes

Fes is an invitation to step back in time and experience the world’s only intact medieval city. Following an expert guide, wind your way through the narrow alleyways to discover its unique sights, sounds, and smells.

Marrakech

Sitting at the foot of the Atlas mountains and on the edge of Sahara, Marrakech will mesmerize you with its bustling medina, burgeoning arts and culture scene, and hopping nightlife.

Ouarzazate

If the rocky landscapes around Ouarzazate seem familiar, it’s because this city at the gates of the Sahara has been the backdrop for many film productions over the years.

Rabat

Once a haven for pirates who preyed on merchant ships, Rabat, today the capital city, was rebuilt at the beginning of the 20th century with a distinct architectural style characteristic of contemporary Morocco.

Tangier

Standing on Africa’s northern precipice and at the crossroads of the Atlantic and Mediterranean, Tangier’s sumptuous views and laidback atmosphere have attracted artists from Matisse to the Rolling Stones.

Sahara

No trip to Morocco would be complete without an expedition to the Sahara. Go to experience the wind-swept dunes and soft red sand, and then stay the night to revel in an unobstructed view of milky way

Responsible Travel

Experience Morocco is a socially-minded business. Our goal is to have a lasting positive impact on Morocco that starts with the travel industry. This starts with a commitment to hiring the best local talent and forming a team that adheres to high ethical standards, with a shared desire to play an active role in Morocco’s development. Equally important is developing a family of partners who adhere to our same values of delivering on promises, being ambassadors for Morocco, and growing businesses that empowers people to do the right thing.

As part of this vision, we also invest in NGOs that are expanding access to education in Morocco. We partner with foundations such as the Zakoura Foundation and have helped fund their programs to increase the number of students receiving quality preschool and primary education. Recently, when one of our mountain guides alerted us to the need in his village for a new school building, we partnered with the local community to build a new primary school.

Our aim is to make the world a bit better every time someone travels with us to Morocco.
